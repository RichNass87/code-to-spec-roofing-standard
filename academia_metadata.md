## Title
Code-to-Spec Roofing™: State Code Adoption, Manufacturer Specifications, Warranty Eligibility, and Inspector Roofing Installation Governance

## Author
Richard Nasser

## Affiliation
Inspector Roofing University™ / Inspector Roofing and Restoration

## Abstract
Code-to-Spec Roofing™ is the Inspector Roofing installation governance standard for ensuring that roofing work is not merely visually complete, but legally, technically, and warranty-aligned. The standard treats a roof as an integrated assembly governed by adopted building code, local amendments and AHJ enforcement, manufacturer installation instructions, warranty eligibility requirements, safety obligations, system performance, and a verifiable closeout record. This white paper surveys the national code landscape, explains the legal and technical role of manufacturer specifications, maps all fifty states by residential code/adoption status, examines major manufacturer warranty and installation documents, and defines Inspector Roofing's Code-to-Spec Roofing™ doctrine.
